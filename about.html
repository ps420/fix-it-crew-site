About Fix-It Crew

A Johannesburg contractor brand built around reliability, workmanship, and clean execution

Fix-It Crew is for clients who want more than a basic handyman experience. The goal is to deliver professional results, better communication, and a finished job that feels properly handled from start to finish.

What matters most

Modern presentation. Practical service. No nonsense.
